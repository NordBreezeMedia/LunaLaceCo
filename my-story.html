Brittany Nantel – Founder & Co-Founder of LunaLace Co.

Empowerment, confidence, and gorgeous lingerie for every body.

Hi, I’m Brittany Nantel, the founder and co-founder of LunaLace Co. I started this business after coming out of a toxic relationship and learning how to love my body again. My mission is to help women feel empowered, confident, and beautiful in their own skin through gorgeous lingerie.

At LunaLace Co., we believe every body deserves options—including plus-size styles—without shame or hesitation. I know firsthand the journey of body transformation: I was over 230 lbs and lost the weight through a major personal health journey, learning self-love along the way. My partner has been my biggest supporter, and that encouragement fuels everything I do.

I want women to feel good, look good, and not overspend on something that should celebrate them. Based in Montreal, Canada, and the Sunshine Coast, BC, our pieces are not just stunning—they’re also perfect for self-care and meditation, which is a big part of my life.

At LunaLace Co., we’re about empowerment, comfort, and gorgeous lingerie for every body.
